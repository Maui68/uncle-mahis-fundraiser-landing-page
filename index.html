Our Story

A Family Recipe,
Passed Down with Aloha.

Uncle Mahi's Kūlolo started in the 1970s when Uncle JJ Mahi crafted a kūlolo unlike anything else on the island. That same recipe, and the same care behind it, has been carried forward for three generations.

  • 1970s
    Uncle JJ Mahi
    Started it all with a one-of-a-kind recipe born from Maui kalo and family tradition.
  • 2013
    John Mahi
    Uncle JJ's son took over, growing the business through roadside stands and local stores across Maui.
  • February 2026
    A New Generation
    The current owner steps in to continue the family legacy, keeping every slab as authentic as the first.
What is Kūlolo?

A Hawaiian Treasure
You'll Never Forget.

Kūlolo is one of Hawaii's most treasured, and hardest to find, traditional desserts. Made from freshly grated Maui taro and coconut, it requires an extensive preparation and cooking process that most aren't willing to do. That's why you rarely see it, and why truly great kūlolo is even rarer.


Dense, rich, and naturally sweet, every piece of Uncle Mahi's Kūlolo is worth the wait, and worth every bite.

What's In Every Slab

  • 13 oz. of pure, handcrafted kūlolo
  • No preservatives. Ever.
  • Rare by nature
  • Hard to find, impossible to forget
